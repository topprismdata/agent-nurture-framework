## Self-Evolving Agents

30. Zhou, A. et al. (2025). "Darwin Gödel Machine: Open-Ended Evolution of Self-Improving Agents." *arXiv preprint*. Introduced in the context of self-evolving agent surveys, the Darwin Gödel Machine (DGM) concept proposes that agents can recursively improve their own architecture by searching over code space, validating improvements through empirical evaluation---an approach complementary to NFD's knowledge-level self-improvement through crystallization.
